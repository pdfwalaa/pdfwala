Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

Simple version: PDFWala processes all your PDF files directly in your browser. We do not upload, store, or share your files or personal data with anyone.

Introduction

Welcome to PDFWala ("we", "our", or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our website at pdfwala.site.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal information to operate our service:

  • Usage Data: Basic analytics such as pages visited, browser type, and device type — collected anonymously.
  • Files: PDF files you use with our tools are processed entirely in your browser and are never uploaded to our servers.
  • Cookies: We may use cookies to remember your preferences (such as dark/light mode).

How We Use Information

  • To provide and improve our PDF tools
  • To analyze usage and fix bugs
  • To display relevant advertisements (via Google AdSense)

Google AdSense & Third-Party Ads

We use Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies to show personalized ads based on your browsing history. You can opt out at Google Ad Settings.

For more information, refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

Data Storage & Security

PDFWala does not store your PDF files on any server. All file processing happens locally in your browser. We do not have access to the content of your documents.

Third-Party Services

Our website may use the following third-party services:

  • Google Analytics (anonymous usage tracking)
  • Google AdSense (advertising)
  • Cloudflare (CDN and security)

Children's Privacy

PDFWala does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy occasionally. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of our service means you accept the updated policy.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, feel free to reach out via our Contact page.
